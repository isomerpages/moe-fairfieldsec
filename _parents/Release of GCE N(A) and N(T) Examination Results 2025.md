<h2><strong>2025 GCE N-Level Result Release</strong></h2>
<p>The 2025 GCE N-Level results will be released on <strong>18 December 2025 (Thursday) at 2pm.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong><u>Result release information:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Date: 18 December 2025 (Thursday)
<br>Reporting Time: 1.30pm*
<br>Venue: CSL Hall
<br>Attire: School polo shirt with pants/skirts or full school uniform</p>
<p>*Briefing on JIE/DPP/PFP will begin at 1.45pm, and results will be released
at 2pm.</p>
<p>Students who turn up in improper attire and/or with coloured hair will
not be allowed to collect their results with the rest of the students.
They will instead only be able to collect their results from the General
Office after the main group has collected their results.</p>
<p>All students are to return to their Secondary schools to collect their
results. Students who are unable to collect their results in person may
appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results from school.

verification when collecting the results. The proxy form can be downloaded
