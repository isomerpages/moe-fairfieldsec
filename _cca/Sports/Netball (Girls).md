<label for="accordion2">Overview</label>
<div>

<p>
<b>Vision:</b><br> 
To be a choice CCA in Fairfield for girls who wish to learn and grow through Netball as a sport.<br>

<b>Mission:</b><br> 

To promote and develop the values of:<br>

• resilience<br>
• respect for all (team mates, teachers, coach and opponents)<br>
• fair play<br>
• leadership<br>
• a winning spirit<br>
	
through our regular training sessions, competitions, friendly matches and camps.<br>

<b>History</b><br> 

Besides developing in competency and skills of the game, Fairfield Netball CCA seeks to promote and develop the values they need throughout life.<br><br>


A winning spirit directs the team towards the same goal of achieving success through winning but the emphasis is never just about winning a match. Through competitions and friendly matches, our girls will gain opportunities to play fairly, develop and demonstrate respect for teammates as well as their opponents, and care for each other as a team.<br><br>


It is our hope that these values will build their mental strength that can help the girls to overcome any obstacles and challenges that they face in their studies and in their lives after graduating from Fairfield.<br><br>


Many of our girls started without any experience in competitive sports. Netball CCA provides a safe and supportive environment for players to step up and lead their level or the team. We welcome girls who are passionate about the sport and are willing to learn and grow together.<br><br>

  

<b>Highlights</b><br> 

• Participation in annual National School Games Netball South Zone Championship<br>
• Participation in annual Sneakers Netball Challenge organised by Netball Singapore (opened to all secondary schools in Singapore)<br>
• Participation in annual Deloitte Pesta Sukan organised by Netball Singapore (opened to all secondary schools in Singapore)<br>
• Friendly matches with other schools
