<p>Overview</p>
<p><strong>Vision &amp; Mission</strong>
<br>
</p>
<p>With the 3 As – Attitude, Attire and Attendance – serving as our core
values, we strive to nurture our players to achieve excellence, not just
in the sport, but also in their academic pursuit and personal lives.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Key Programmes / History</strong>
<br>
</p>
<p>Besides our regular trainings, we also organize friendly matches and other
outdoor activities like camps, and VIAs so that our players will become
effective teammates and influential leaders who display resilience, sportsmanship,
discipline and integrity.</p>
