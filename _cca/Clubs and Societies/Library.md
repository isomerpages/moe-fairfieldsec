<b>Vision & Mission</b><br> 

The Fairfield's Media Resource Library or Library@Fairfield aims to publicise and promote the use of books and media resources for educational, entertainment purposes and to provide quality services for users.<br><br>

  

<b> History</b><br>

• To nurture students to be information literate <br>
    
-    access information efficiently and effectively<br>
    
-    evaluate information critically and competently<br>
    
-    apply information effectively and creatively<br><br>
    

• To nurture students to be independent learners <br>
    
-    pursue information related to personal interests<br>
    
-    appreciate and enjoy literature and other creative expressions of information<br>
    
-    strive for excellence in information seeking and knowledge generation<br><br>
    

• To nurture students who contributes positively to the learning community <br>
    
-    exhibit ethical behaviour with regards to information and information technology<br>
    
-    participate effectively in groups to pursue and generate information.<br><br>
    

  

<b>Highlights</b><br>

• Monthly theme book displays<br>
• Monthly book reviews<br>
• Values-in-action programme during the March holidays<br>
• Teacher’s Day gift making<br>
• Day camp (after End-of-year examinations)	<br>
