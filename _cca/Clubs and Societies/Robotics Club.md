<label for="accordion2">Overview</label>

<div>

<p>
<b>Vision</b><Br>

To create an inclusive community where everyone is treated fairly and with respect.<Br><Br>

  

<b>Mission</b><Br>

To empower every members of Robotics@Fairfield to push past their limits and plan for the future.<Br><Br>

  

<b>History</b><Br>

In FRC, we use LEGO Mindstorms to teach students the two core components of Robotics - building and programming.<Br><Br>

  

Robotics is an effective way to teach programming as students can see the robot in action and learn that precise programming instructions are required.<Br><Br>

  

As a STEM topic, students can also develop valuable soft skills, such as the ability to think critically in stressful situations, time management and conflict resolution.<Br><Br>

  

FRC’s syllabus is not only Robotics- based. CCA leaders will organise VIA activities which includes community work with various organisations.<Br><Br>

  

FRC participates in two to three competitions each year- IDE Series*, NRPC and NRC. Recently, FRC won 1st in NRPC 2019 (Robot Performance, Technical Presentation).<Br><Br>

  

<b>Key Competitions</b><Br>

1)  National Robotics and Programming competition (NRPC)<Br>

2)  National Robotics Competition (NRC)
