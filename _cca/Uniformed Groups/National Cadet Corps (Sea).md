<p>
<b>Mission</b><Br><Br>

To nurture inspiring leaders and committed citizens through fun, adventurous and military-related activities  <Br><Br>

  

<b>Vision</b><Br><Br>

Extraordinary Youth Leaders<Br><Br>

  

<b>History</b><Br><Br>

Fairfield National Cadet Corps (Sea) unit started in 2006 as a Mixed Unit.<Br><Br>

  

NCC (Sea) aims to develop all cadets holistically through a variety of military-related and water-based activities. The NCC curriculum provides cadets with enriching learning experiences, which build upon the pillars of the NCC Cadet Strong framework: <b><i>Fit Body, Resilient Mind and Committed Heart</i></b>.<Br><Br>

  

Being a CCA filled with numerous adventurous activities, the cadets are constantly reminded of the importance of having a <b><i>Fit Body</i></b> so that they can enjoy these fun-filled activities to the fullest. Through structured physical training sessions and inculcating a sense of discipline, our cadets takes the ownership to build up their physical fitness.<Br><Br>

  

Through the rigorous training for sea sports such as Dragon Boating and Kayaking, cadets not only hone their skills in water sports, but also develop a <b><i>Resilient Mind</i></b>. This is an important life skill which keeps our cadets going in challenging times as we nurture them into Extraordinary Youth Leaders.<Br><Br>

  

The shared experience during training sessions and annual camps build a strong sense of camaraderie among the cadets. It takes teamwork to make the dream work. The unit’s achievements are testaments of our cadets’ and teacher officers’ <b><i>Committed Hearts</i></b> towards the unit and collective aspiration to scale greater heights.<Br><Br>
