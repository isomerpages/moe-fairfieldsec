<p>
<b>Mission</b><br> 
To nurture inspiring leaders and committed citizens through fun, adventurous and military-related activities<br><br>

  

<b>Vision</b><br> 
Extraordinary Youth Leaders<br><br>

  

<b>History</b><br> 

Fairfield National Cadet Corps (Sea) unit started in 2006 as a Mixed Unit.<br>
 

NCC (Sea) aims to develop all cadets holistically through a variety of military-related and water-based activities. The NCC curriculum provides cadets with enriching learning experiences, which build upon the pillars of the NCC Cadet Strong framework:&nbsp;<b><i>Fit Body, Resilient Mind and Committed Heart</i></b>.<br><br>

  

Being a CCA filled with numerous adventurous activities, the cadets are constantly reminded of the importance of having a&nbsp;<b><i>Fit Body</i></b>&nbsp;so that they can enjoy these fun-filled activities to the fullest. Through structured physical training sessions and inculcating a sense of discipline, our cadets takes the ownership to build up their physical fitness.<br in="" for=""><i>Resilient Mind</i>. This is an important life skill which keeps our cadets going in challenging times as we nurture them into Extraordinary Youth Leaders.<br><i>Committed Hearts</i>&nbsp;towards the unit and collective aspiration to scale greater heights.<br><br>
