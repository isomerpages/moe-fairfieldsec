<p>Overview</p>
<p><strong>Our Objective</strong>
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p>The Object of the Boys’ Brigade shall be the advancement of Christ Kingdom
amongst Boys and the promotion of habits of Obedience, Reverence, Discipline,
Self-Respect and all that tends towards a true Christian Manliness.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Our Mission</strong>
<br>Growing Boys to serve &amp; lead, preaching Christ the BB way.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Our Vision</strong>
<br>To be a family of God-fearing Boys, passionate in making a difference.
<br>
</p>
<p>For more info, do visit our 33rd&nbsp;Company Instagram:
<br>
</p>
<p><a href="https://www.instagram.com/33rdbbcoy/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.instagram.com/33rdbbcoy/</a>

<p><strong>History</strong>
<br>The Boys' Brigade was founded by Sir William Alexander Smith on 4 October
1883 in Glasgow, Scotland. The BB was first introduced to Singapore in
1930 by Mr. James Milner Fraser, and is recognized as a Uniformed Organization
by the Ministry of Education. The Patron of the BB in Singapore is none
other than His Excellency, the President of the Republic of Singapore.
<br>
</p>
<p>The 33rd Singapore Company was first started in Fairfield Methodist Secondary
School in 1983, when Fairfield became co-educational. Mr. Jimmy Png was
the first Captain of the 33rd Company. Together with a group of Officers
and Seniors from the 25th Company, they began to build a new company from
scratch. Mr. Wee Tat Chuen assumed leadership of the Company as the Captain
in 1996. Under his leadership the Company grew from strength to strength.
After being the Captain for 7 years, Mr. Wee passed the baton to Mr. Glen
Tan in 2003. Mr. Glen Tan then passed the Captainship to Mr. Joel Li in
2009. In 2015, Mr. Joel Lai took over as Captain. A new 33rd vision was
initiated by Mr. Joel Lai - to be a “family of God-fearing Boys, passionate
in making a difference.” We currently have about 120 Boys, 10 Officers
and 11 Primers.
